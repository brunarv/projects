Who am I?

My name is Bruna Ramos Vieira and my student number is A00985171. I am from Brazil and I came to Vancouver almost two years ago. Having the opportunity to live abroad was always a dream for me, however I wanted more than an experience of an exchange, I wanted to do something that could contribute in my career, that's why I decided to enroll in the Applied Software Development (ASD) and Applied Web Development (AWD) programs at BCIT. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Information System from Pontifícia Universidade Católica (Campinas/SP - Brazil) and I am an IBQTS Foundation Level certified tester.

What do I do?

I have been working as a Software Quality Assurance Analyst for 6 years. I have an experience in all the different testing phases in the software lifecycle. My work experience involved all the parts of information systems and software testing: test plan elaboration, test case creation, environment preparation, execution and report creation, defect management, execution management. I have performed in projects for Printers, Notebooks, Tablets, Mobile Phone and SmartWatch device with methodology scrum.
