One of my favorite singers

Shania Twain

I grew up with Shania Twain's music. She is Canadian, as I am living in Canada currently, that's why I decided to describe my passion for this great singer.

One of the best voice, the best performer, and a great person. She's a music legend. I love her voice, her thoughts, the way her dressing and everything about her. She's a great woman!

Two years ago I had the great pleasure to watch her concert for the first time in my live. It was in Las Vegas and definitely was an unforgettable day.
