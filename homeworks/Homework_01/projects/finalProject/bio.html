Chrystian Ramos was born on July 22, 1997 in São Paulo where he lived until his eight-year-old during this period moved to Valinhos, where his musical career gained more prominence.

Chrystian always liked to play the guitar, and learned even as a child by encouragement from his father, who noticed his son's talent with the instrument. Chrystian began his career in music making presentations in schools, churches and restaurants.

Besides the guitar, Chrystian plays viola and twelve-string guitar. He is a multi-instrumentalist. And it is with much humility, simplicity and talent that boy still have much to show what he can do!
