San Francisco

One of the cities I enjoyed the most in my travels around the world was San Francisco, California. In my case, I stayed for only a week in the city. San Francisco is a city with many cool attractions and putting together a script in this incredible California city is not easy. I stayed for a week in the city and I knew a little bit of everything: the bay with the sea lions, Alcatraz Island, parks, churches, museums, houses with super different architecture, and more.
