Who am I?

My name is Bruna Ramos Vieira. I am a woman in love for life, family, friends, animals and technology. Someone that wake up every day and feel happy for being alive, to have a new opportunity to learn and provide something good for who are around me whether in life or at work. I am someone with sense of ownership and passion for quality.


Travel Lover, that's me! I am from Brazil and I came to Vancouver almost two years ago. Having the opportunity to live abroad was always a dream for me, however I wanted more than an experience of an exchange, I wanted to do something that could contribute in my career, that's why I decided to enroll in the Applied Software Development (ASD) and Applied Web Development (AWD) programs at BCIT. And so far, it has been a great journey full of challenges!


I hold a Bachelor's degree in Information System from Pontifícia Universidade Católica (Campinas/SP - Brazil) and I am an IBQTS Foundation Level certified tester.

What do I do?

I have been working as a Software Quality Assurance Analyst for 6 years and also I have worked in some projects as Web Developer.

Regarding my background in Software Testing, I have experience in all different testing phases in the software lifecycle. My work experience involved all the parts of information systems and software testing:

    Test plan elaboration
    Test case creation
    Environment preparation
    Execution and report creation
    Defect management
    Execution management

I have performed in projects for Printers, Notebooks, Tablets, Mobile Phone and SmartWatch device with methodology scrum.
